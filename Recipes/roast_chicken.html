Gourmet Roast Chicken

Description:

Roast chicken is chicken prepared as food by roasting whether in a home kitchen, over a fire, or with a rotisserie (rotary spit). Generally, the chicken is roasted with its own fat and juices by circulating the meat during roasting, and therefore, are usually cooked exposed to fire or heat with some type of rotary grill so that the circulation of these fats and juices is as efficient as possible.

Roast chicken is a dish that appears in a wide variety of cuisines worldwide.

Ingredients Required:

  • 1 Whole Chicken
  • Salt, Maldon Sea Salt and Ground Black Pepper
  • 1 tablespoon Onion Powder
  • Ginger-Garlic Paste, and fine minced Garlic, and large, thin garlic slices
  • Chicken Stock
  • Onions Chopped (Big Slices), and finely chopped onions
  • Bell Peppers
  • Cumin Powder
  • Coriander Powder
  • Kasuri Methi
  • Large Chopped tomatoes
  • Dark Brown Sugar
  • Red Chilli Powder
  • A cooking oil
  • Deep Frying oil
  • 1 lemon, juiced
  • Yoghurt
  • Any spicy chillies
  • Thyme, Rosemary, Cinnamon Powder powdered and in a spice bag
  • Lemon Grass
  • Potato Starch

Steps:

  1. Marinating the Chicken:

    1. Score the Chicken, make the scoring on the breast light, and the thighs deep
    2. Add the Chicken into a container
    3. Add enough Yoghurt to cover the whole chicken
    4. Add the lemon juice, chilli powder, Ginger-Garlic paste with Spicy Chilli, Cumin Powder, Onion Powder, Mixed Spice Powder into the container
    5. Mix the ingredients and massage it into the Chicken
    6. Remove the gizzards, neck and heart
    7. Add a little marinade to the inside as well, and add the spice bag and bruised lemon grass
    8. Marinade the organs separately and add potato starch and more spices, with less yoghurt
    9. Leave both aside for 2-24 hours

  2. Prepping the Oven: Preheat the Oven to 300 degrees.
  3. Add the Chicken onto a greased baking sheet on a baking tray with the onions, capsicum, tomatoes and some chicken-stock and slide into the pre-heated oven
  4. Bake for 25 minutes
  5. Once Onions and Capsicums are Roasted, remove chicken from oven, take the Capsicums, Onions, Onions and the juice out.
  6. Rest the chicken for 10 minutes at room temperature
  7. Remove the over-charred bits of the onions capsicum and tomatoes and add to a blender with Chicken stock, Dark Brown Sugar and some chilli-powder.
  8. Blend till thick, and add to a heated sauce-pan.
  9. Ladle some of the sauce out for later. Cook the rest of the sauce till thick.
  10. Crank the oven up to 450 degrees and bake for 10 minutes to crisp up the skin
  11. Take out at minute 6, add the ladled sauce on it, and add to the oven at 500 degrees
  12. Take the chicken out and Rest for 10 more minutes.
  13. Meanwhile prep a pan to fry in.
  14. Deep fry the large garlic slices until very crunchy.
  15. Then deep fry the other parts of the chicken for 3-4 minutes.
  16. Separate the parts of the chicken by Legs, Thigh, Breast and Wings.
  17. Serve with a garnish of fried garlic, with a drizzle of sauce and a side of fried gizzards.
